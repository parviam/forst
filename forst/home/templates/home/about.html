About Us

Forst is a community gardening platform that connects people with local gardens and gardening resources. Our mission is to promote sustainable gardening practices and foster a sense of community through gardening.

We believe that everyone should have access to green spaces and the opportunity to grow their own food. Whether you're a seasoned gardener or just starting out, Forst is here to help you on your gardening journey.

Join us today and start growing your own garden!
